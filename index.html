About Me

I have been currently working as a Senior Lecturer at Khwopa Engineering College, Purbanchal University, Nepal. I also worked as a lecturer at school of engineering, Pokhara University, Nepal. Before this, I worked in software industries as senior programmer. I have more than 6 years of working experience in software development. So, I have working experience in both academic as well as software industries.

I received my Ph.D. in computer science and technology from the Key Laboratory of Network Data Science & Technology, Institute of Computing Technology, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (ICT-UCAS), Beijing, China in 2020. I worked there under the supervision of Professor Xueqi Cheng. The focus of my PhD was in the domain of Machine Learning (ML), Deep Learning (DL), Reinforcement Learning (RL) and Bigdata, especially in parameter optimization & variable selection, online and off-line policy evaluation in RL. I completed my master of engineering (M.E.) degree in computer science and technology from Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT), Beijing, China under supervision of professor Yanlong Zhai in 2015. I published a conference paper as a first author in WASA 2014 international conference at Harbin, China during my master study. My master research lied in Bigdata. Prior to that, I received my BS in computer science degree from National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences (NUCES-FAST), Pakistan in 2005. I am also an officially oracle certified professional (OCP) DBA and Oracle certified associate (OCA) Developer.

I am enthusiastic about research, eager to acquire new knowledge and explore the field of software engineering, programming languages, database, and artificial intelligence. My research interests include Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Reinforcement Learning, Computer Vision, and Bigdata etc.
